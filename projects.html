Hand-Mounted Flamethrower (25th September 2020)

Inspired by Allen Pan, this hand mounted flamethrower mimics the firebending abilities of the Avatar from the show “The Last Airbender”. It makes use a pressurised butane canister and the circuit from an arc lighter.
Watch the video here: https://youtu.be/V5g7BbxaJ8A
Time spent: 2 days

Lightsaber (12th September 2020)

The lightsaber uses methanol as fuel, contained within a high pressure turkey marinade injector. Using a syringe piston, the butane and methanol are separated. Methanol causes blindness so this project was scary.
Time spent: 3 months

Motorised toy wheelchair for GoBabyGo!(14th April 2018)

We modified an electric car for the kids at AWWA, making it disability friendly. The seats are custom molded for easy clean up, and reprogrammed using an arduino to be operated by a single button instead of steering wheel
Time spent: 16 months
